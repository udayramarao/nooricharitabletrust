Health Camps

Our health camps bring vital medical services to underserved rural and urban communities. We organize regular camps offering free consultations, diagnostic tests, and essential medications. Led by experienced doctors and volunteers, these camps address common health issues, provide preventive care education, and refer critical cases to hospitals. Since 2020, we’ve conducted over 50 camps, serving more than 10,000 individuals.

×

Women Empowerment

Our women empowerment programs focus on education, skill development, and emotional support. We provide vocational training in tailoring, handicrafts, and entrepreneurship, enabling women to achieve financial independence. Additionally, we conduct workshops on health, legal rights, and leadership, fostering confidence and community leadership. Over 1,000 women have benefited from our initiatives since inception.

×

Environment

Our environmental initiatives aim to create a sustainable future. We organize tree-planting drives, having planted over 5,000 trees across communities. Our clean-up campaigns target rivers, parks, and streets, raising awareness about waste management. We also educate schools and communities on eco-friendly practices, contributing to a greener planet.

×

Feeding Animals

We care for stray and abandoned animals by providing food, medical care, and shelter. Our volunteers distribute meals daily to street dogs and cats, while our veterinary camps offer vaccinations and treatments. We’ve supported over 2,000 animals, promoting compassion and coexistence in communities.

×

COVID-19 Pandemic

During the COVID-19 crisis, we distributed free medications, oxygen kits, and food supplies to thousands of affected families. Our team worked tirelessly in high-risk areas, providing PPE kits to frontline workers and raising awareness about safety protocols. Our efforts reached over 15,000 individuals during the pandemic’s peak.

×

Orphans

Our orphan care program provides a safe haven for children without families. We offer shelter, nutritious meals, education, and emotional support through our orphanage. Our dedicated staff ensures each child receives personalized care, helping them build a brighter future. Currently, we support over 100 children with holistic care.

Dr. Noori Parveen: The ₹10 Doctor with a Heart of Gold

In the heart of India, where hope often battles hardship, shines the inspiring story of Dr. Noori Parveen, fondly known as the “₹10 Doctor.” A General Physician by profession and a humanitarian by heart, Dr. Noori has been a beacon of light for the underprivileged since 2016.

Raised with values rooted in compassion and service, Noori always dreamed of healing more than just bodies—she wanted to heal communities. In 2020, after years of dedicated work, she officially registered her dream as Noori’s Hospitals, a charitable trust aimed at providing affordable healthcare to all. Her unique initiative of charging just ₹10 for consultations earned her the title “₹10 Doctor,” a symbol of accessible healthcare for the poor.

But Dr. Noori’s impact reaches far beyond hospital walls.

She runs an orphanage home, offering love, education, and shelter to children who have no one else. Her hands, though constantly busy healing patients, also find time to plant trees, clean streets, and care for animals. Through environmental campaigns and awareness drives, she has nurtured not only lives but also the earth.

From organizing free health camps in remote villages to distributing books and clothes to the needy, Noori’s life is a living example of selflessness. During the harrowing days of the COVID-19 pandemic, while many stayed behind closed doors, she stepped out—fearless and determined. She treated thousands, offered food and essentials to struggling families, and gave a voice to the voiceless.

Her story doesn’t end there. Dr. Noori regularly holds motivational talks for poor children and struggling communities, encouraging them to believe in education, hope, and hard work. To them, she’s not just a doctor—she’s a guide, a sister, a savior.

In a world often driven by profit, Dr. Noori Parveen stands as a powerful reminder that kindness still exists. Her work is a living testimony that one woman with a strong will and a compassionate heart can change countless lives.

She is not just a doctor.
She is a movement.
She is Dr. Noori Parveen—the doctor who treats with medicine and heals with love.
